Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between diamond weave and professional grade cords?

Diamond weave cords (261 lbs capacity) are designed for shock absorption and general hold-down applications. Professional grade cords (555 lbs capacity) offer maximum strength for heavy-duty commercial use like securing cargo to bike trailers.

Can you make custom lengths?

Yes! We specialize in custom length orders. Whether you need 8 inches or 8 feet, we can manufacture bungee cords to your exact specifications.

What's your minimum order quantity for bulk orders?

For bulk pricing, we typically start at 100 units. However, we can accommodate smaller quantities for custom specifications. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How long does custom manufacturing take?

Custom orders typically take 1-2 weeks to manufacture, depending on specifications and quantity. We'll provide an exact timeline with your quote.
